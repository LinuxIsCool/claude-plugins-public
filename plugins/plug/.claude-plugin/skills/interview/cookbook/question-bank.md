## Discovery Domain (Default)

### Opening Questions
- What brings you here today? What's the main thing you're trying to accomplish?
- Can you describe the current state of things? What's working and what isn't?
- Who are the key stakeholders or users affected by this?

### Exploration Questions
- What does success look like for this initiative?
- What constraints are you operating under (time, budget, technical, organizational)?
- What's been tried before? What did you learn from it?
- What are the biggest risks or concerns you have?
- What trade-offs are you willing to make? What's non-negotiable?
- Who else has opinions on this that we should consider?
- What information or resources do you have access to?

### Closing Questions
- What would make this a clear success in your eyes?
- Is there anything we haven't discussed that feels important?
- What's your ideal next step after this conversation?

## Product Domain

### Opening Questions
- Who is the primary user for this product/feature?
- What problem are you solving for them?
- How do users currently solve this problem (if at all)?

### Exploration Questions
- What's the core user journey you're designing for?
- What's the minimum viable version of this?
- How will users discover this feature/product?
- What competing solutions exist? How is this different?
- What metrics will tell you if this is successful?
- What feedback have you received from users so far?
- What's the rollout strategy?

### Closing Questions
- If you had to ship something this week, what would it be?
- What's the one thing that would make users love this?
- What keeps you up at night about this product?

## Technical Domain

### Opening Questions
- What's the current technical architecture?
- What technologies/stack are you working with?
- What are the main technical challenges you're facing?

### Exploration Questions
- What are the performance requirements (latency, throughput, scale)?
- What are the reliability requirements (uptime, data integrity)?
- What security considerations are there?
- How does this integrate with existing systems?
- What's the testing strategy?
- What's the deployment/release process?
- What technical debt exists that we should be aware of?
- What monitoring and observability do you have?

### Closing Questions
- What technical decisions do you need to make soon?
- Where do you need the most help technically?
- What would make the engineering team's life easier?

---

## Strategic Domain

### Opening Questions
- What's the big picture vision here?
- How does this fit into broader organizational goals?
- What's driving the timing of this initiative?

### Exploration Questions
- What resources (people, budget, time) are allocated?
- Who are the decision-makers and what are their priorities?
- What's the competitive landscape?
- What happens if this succeeds? What happens if it doesn't?
- How will you communicate progress and results?
- What organizational changes might this require?
- What are the key milestones and checkpoints?

### Closing Questions
- What's the decision you need to make in the next week?
- What would need to be true for you to feel confident moving forward?
- What support do you need from others to succeed?

## Plugin/Tool Development Domain

### Opening Questions
- What capability or workflow are you trying to enable?
- Who will use this plugin/tool and when?
- What does the current workflow look like without this?

### Exploration Questions
- What's the trigger for using this tool? (command, hook, automatic)
- What inputs does it need? Where do they come from?
- What outputs should it produce? What format?
- How should it handle errors or edge cases?
- What existing tools or patterns should it integrate with?
- How should it be configured or customized?
- What permissions or access does it need?

### Closing Questions
- What's the simplest version that would be useful?
- What would make you use this every day?
- What would make this feel magical vs. just functional?

## Adaptive Question Templates

These templates can be filled based on context:

**Clarification**
- When you say "{X}", what specifically do you mean by that?
- Can you give me an example of "{X}"?

**Prioritization**
- If you could only have one of {A, B, C}, which would it be?
- What would you be willing to give up to get {X}?

**Boundary Testing**
- What would make this project a failure?
- What's definitely out of scope?

**Stakeholder Mapping**
- Who else cares about this? What do they want?
- Who might resist this? Why?

**Temporal**
- What needs to happen first before {X} can work?
- What's the timeline you're working with?

**Emotional**
- What excites you most about this?
- What worries you the most?

## Question Selection Heuristics

1. **Start broad, narrow as you learn**
2. **Follow energy** - dig into what the user is enthusiastic or concerned about
3. **Challenge assumptions** - gently probe when something seems taken for granted
4. **Balance positive and negative** - explore both hopes and fears
5. **Get concrete** - ask for examples when answers are abstract
6. **Respect the budget** - save consolidation questions for the end
