# Compliance

Corporate compliance requirements for Canadian businesses, with focus on BC and federal filings.

## Corporate Registry Requirements

### BC Corporate Registry

**Annual Report**:
- Due: Anniversary of incorporation
- Fee: ~$45
- Contents: Confirm or update director/registered office info
- Late Fee: Additional penalties apply

**Address Change**:
- File Notice of Change of Address
- Within 15 days of change

**Director Changes**:
- File Notice of Change of Directors
- Within 15 days of change

### Federal (Corporations Canada)

**Annual Return**:
- Due: Within 60 days of anniversary
- Fee: ~$20 online, $40 paper
- Contents: Confirm or update corporate information

**Beneficial Ownership Registry**:
- New requirement (recent implementation)
- Individuals with significant control
- Must keep register, file annually

### Extra-Provincial Registration

If operating in provinces other than incorporation:
- Register in each province
- Maintain agent/office in province
- Annual filings in each province
- Different names possible in some provinces

## Tax Filings

### Corporate Tax (T2)

**Due Date**: 6 months after fiscal year-end
**Payment Due**:
- 2 months after year-end (most corporations)
- 3 months after year-end (CCPC claiming SBD, if certain conditions met)

**Schedules Commonly Required**:
- Schedule 1: Net Income Reconciliation
- Schedule 3: Dividends Received, Paid
- Schedule 4: Losses
- Schedule 7: GRIP (if applicable)
- Schedule 8: CCA (depreciation)
- Schedule 50: Shareholder Information
- Schedule 100-141: Financial statement data

**Input Tax Credits**: Claim within 4 years

### Payroll Remittances

**Source Deductions** (CPP, EI, Income Tax):
- Must withhold from employee pay
- Remit to CRA
- Frequency based on average monthly withholdings
- Directors personally liable for unremitted amounts

**T4s**: Due end of February for prior year

### PST (BC)

**If applicable**: Collect and remit PST on taxable goods
**Filing frequency**: Based on annual PST collected

## Corporate Records (Minute Book)

### Required Contents

**BC Corporation**:
- Articles of incorporation
- Notice of articles
- Bylaws (or Table 1 defaults)
- Central securities register
- Register of directors
- Register of transfers
- Minutes of shareholder meetings
- Minutes of director meetings
- Written resolutions

### Best Practices

**Annual Housekeeping**:
- Director resolution approving financial statements
- Director resolution declaring dividends (if any)
- Shareholder AGM or unanimous consent
- Election/confirmation of directors
- Appointment of officers (if applicable)
- Update registers if changes

**When Changes Occur**:
- Share issuances/transfers: Update securities register
- Director changes: Update register, file with registry
- Address changes: Update records, file with registry
- Bylaw changes: Proper approval process

### Dividend Documentation

**Required for Dividends**:
- Director resolution declaring dividend
- Date, amount, type (eligible/non-eligible)
- Record date, payment date
- T5 slips to shareholders
- T5 Summary to CRA

## Information Returns

### T5 (Investment Income)

- Issue to shareholders receiving dividends
- Due: End of February
- Report dividend type and gross-up

### T5013 (Partnership)

- If business operated as partnership
- Report each partner's share of income

### T3 (Trust)

- If corporation acts as trustee or has trust arrangements
- Report income allocated to beneficiaries

### T1135 (Foreign Property)

- Required if > $100,000 foreign property
- Due with corporate tax return
- Significant penalties for non-filing

### UHT (Underused Housing Tax)

- New filing requirement for some property holdings
- Exemptions for Canadian corporations meeting criteria
- File annually by April 30

## Regulatory Compliance

### Employment Standards

**BC Employment Standards Act**:
- Minimum wage
- Overtime requirements
- Vacation pay (4% / 6%)
- Statutory holiday pay
- Termination notice/pay

**Required Records**:
- Hours worked
- Pay statements
- Employment records (keep 5+ years)

### Workplace Safety (WorkSafeBC)

**Requirements**:
- Register with WorkSafeBC
- Pay premiums (industry-based rates)
- Report injuries
- Safety requirements vary by industry

### Privacy (PIPA BC / PIPEDA)

**Personal Information Protection**:
- Collect only necessary information
- Consent for collection, use, disclosure
- Safeguard information
- Provide access on request
- Privacy policy recommended

## Penalties and Interest

### Late Filing Penalties

**T2 Corporate Tax**:
- 5% of balance owing + 1% per month (up to 12 months)
- Repeat offenders: higher penalties

**T4/T5 Slips**:
- $100/day minimum, capped based on number

**GST/HST**:
- 1% of balance + 0.25% per month

**BC Annual Report**:
- Late fee applies
- Risk of dissolution for extended non-filing

### Interest

- CRA prescribed rate (changes quarterly)
- Compounds daily
- Applies to all late balances

## Common Compliance Issues

### Share Issuance Without Documentation
- Issue: No director resolution, no updated register
- Fix: Ratify with resolution, update registers

### Missed Annual Filings
- Issue: Corporate registry status at risk
- Fix: File overdue reports, pay penalties

### Dividend Without Documentation
- Issue: No resolution, wrong characterization
- Fix: Document retroactively, correct T5s if needed

### Payroll Non-Compliance
- Issue: Missed remittances
- Fix: Catch up immediately, director liability exposure
