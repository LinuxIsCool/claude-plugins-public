# Canadian Federal

Federal corporate law, taxation, and pan-Canadian business considerations.

## Federal Incorporation

### Canada Business Corporations Act (CBCA)

**Key Features**:
- Nationwide recognition
- 25% Canadian resident director requirement
- Name protection across Canada
- Shareholder proposal rights
- Oppression remedy

**When to Choose Federal**:
- Operating in multiple provinces
- Want "Canada" or "Canadian" in name
- Prestige/perception considerations
- Planning eventual public offering

### Incorporation Process

**Filing Authority**: Corporations Canada (ISED)

**Required Elements**:
- Articles of Incorporation
- NUANS name search report
- Registered office address
- Directors (25% Canadian resident)
- At least one share class

**Costs**:
- Name search: ~$50
- Incorporation: $200 (online) / $250 (paper)
- Annual return: $12

### Director Residency

**Requirement**: At least 25% of directors must be Canadian residents

**Challenges for Foreign Founders**:
- May need to appoint Canadian directors
- Nominee directors raise governance issues
- Consider BC incorporation (no residency requirement)

**Exemptions**: Noneâ€”strict requirement

### Annual Compliance

**Annual Return**: Due within 60 days of anniversary date
- Basic corporate information update
- Filed with Corporations Canada

**Corporate Records**:
- Minute book
- Share register
- Director/officer records
- Financial statements

**No Financial Statement Filing**: For private companies (unlike public companies)

### Shareholder Rights

**Enhanced Rights** (vs. most provincial acts):
- Shareholder proposals at meetings
- Court-ordered compliance
- Robust oppression remedy

**Oppression Remedy**: Powerful tool for minority shareholders
- Broad standing
- Flexible remedies
- Can challenge unfair conduct

### Unanimous Shareholder Agreements

**CBCA USAs**:
- Can transfer all board powers to shareholders
- Shareholders then assume director liability
- Effective for closely-held companies

## Federal Taxation

### Corporate Income Tax

**Federal Rates (2024-2025)**:

| Income Type | Rate |
|-------------|------|
| Small business (CCPC, first $500K) | 9% |
| General active business | 15% |
| Investment income (CCPC) | 38.67% |
| M&P income | 15% |

**Small Business Deduction**:
- Reduces rate from 15% to 9%
- Available to CCPCs
- On first $500K of active business income
- Business limit shared among associated corporations
- Reduced if taxable capital exceeds $10M

### CCPC Status

**Canadian-Controlled Private Corporation**:
- Private corporation
- Not controlled by non-residents
- Not controlled by public corporations
- Shares not listed on exchange

**Benefits**:
- Small business deduction
- Enhanced SR&ED credits
- Stock option deferral
- LCGE access

### Personal Income Tax

**Federal Brackets (2024)**:

| Taxable Income | Rate |
|----------------|------|
| $0 - $55,867 | 15% |
| $55,867 - $111,733 | 20.5% |
| $111,733 - $173,205 | 26% |
| $173,205 - $246,752 | 29% |
| Over $246,752 | 33% |

### GST/HST

**Goods and Services Tax**: 5% federal

**Harmonized Sales Tax**: Federal + provincial combined
- Ontario: 13%
- Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, NL, PEI: 15%

**PST Provinces** (separate from GST):
- BC: 7% PST + 5% GST
- Saskatchewan: 6% PST + 5% GST
- Manitoba: 7% PST + 5% GST

**Quebec**: QST 9.975% + GST 5%

### Registration Thresholds

**GST/HST Registration**:
- Mandatory: >$30,000 revenue in four consecutive quarters
- Voluntary: Can register below threshold

**Input Tax Credits**: Recover GST/HST paid on business expenses

## Federal Programs

### SR&ED (Scientific Research and Experimental Development)

**Federal Credit**:
- CCPC: 35% refundable on first $3M (reduced if prior year income >$500K)
- Others: 15% non-refundable

**Eligible Activities**:
- Basic research
- Applied research
- Experimental development

**Documentation Requirements**:
- Technical: What, why, how, results
- Financial: Labour, materials, contracts, overhead

### IRAP (Industrial Research Assistance Program)

**NRC-IRAP**: Advisory and funding for SMEs

**Offerings**:
- Technical advisory services
- Wage subsidies for R&D staff
- Project funding

**Eligibility**:
- Canadian SME (<500 employees)
- Technology-focused
- Growth-oriented

### Business Development Bank (BDC)

**Services**:
- Loans (complementary to banks)
- Venture capital (BDC Capital)
- Advisory services

**Loan Products**:
- Working capital
- Equipment financing
- Real estate
- Technology financing

### Export Development Canada (EDC)

**For Exporters**:
- Export financing
- Credit insurance
- Bonding
- Foreign investment support

### Canada Small Business Financing Program

**Government-Guaranteed Loans**:
- Up to $1M (including $500K for real property)
- Through financial institutions
- For equipment, leasehold improvements, real property

## Pan-Canadian Operations

### Extra-Provincial Registration

**Requirement**: Must register in each province where "carrying on business"

**Triggers**:
- Physical location
- Employees in province
- Regular sales activity
- Soliciting orders

**Process**: File extra-provincial registration in each province

### Multi-Jurisdictional Compliance

**Employment**:
- Follow law of province where employee works
- Different standards across provinces

**Consumer Protection**:
- Provincial laws apply
- Quebec has distinct requirements

**Privacy**:
- PIPEDA (federal) or provincial equivalent
- Quebec has enhanced requirements (Law 25)

### Provincial Tax Nexus

**Corporate Tax**:
- Allocate income across provinces
- Based on payroll and revenue factors

**Sales Tax**:
- Collect tax where customer located
- Different rules per province

**Industries Under Federal Jurisdiction**:

- Postal services

**Implications**:

- Regulates banks
- Insurance companies
- Trust companies
- Pension plans

**FINTRAC** (Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre):
- Anti-money laundering
- Terrorist financing
- Registration and reporting requirements

## Immigration

### Business Immigration Programs

**Start-up Visa**:
- Commitment from designated VC, angel, or incubator
- Language proficiency
- Sufficient funds
- Permanent residency

**Owner-Operator LMIA**:
- For established business owners
- Create jobs for Canadians
- Path to permanent residency

### Work Permits

**LMIA-Exempt Categories**:
- Global Talent Stream
- Intra-company transfers
- CUSMA professionals

**LMIA Required**:
- Standard work permits
- Must show no Canadian available

**Global Talent Stream**:
- Fast processing (2 weeks)
- For highly skilled tech workers
- Employer must commit to benefits plan

## Competition Law

### Competition Act

**Federal Law**: Applies across Canada

**Key Prohibitions**:
- Price-fixing
- Market allocation
- Bid-rigging
- Abuse of dominance
- Deceptive marketing

### Merger Notification

**Pre-Merger Notification**:
- Transaction >$93M (party assets/revenue in Canada)
- Combined >$400M (assets/revenue in Canada)

**Process**: File with Competition Bureau, waiting period

### Investment Canada Act

**Foreign Investment Review**:
- Direct acquisition of Canadian business >$1.17B (WTO investors)
- Lower thresholds for cultural businesses
- National security review (no threshold)

## Intellectual Property

### Federal IP Registration

**Canadian Intellectual Property Office (CIPO)**:
- Patents
- Trademarks
- Copyright
- Industrial designs

### Patent Protection

**20-Year Term**: From filing date
**Maintenance Fees**: Annual fees years 2-20

### Trademark Protection

**10-Year Term**: Renewable indefinitely
**Canada Brand Database**: Searchable trademark registry

## Comparison with Provincial

### When to Incorporate Federally

**Choose Federal When**:
- Multi-province operations from start
- Want "Canada" in name
- Prestige matters (perception)
- May go public eventually
- Name protection across Canada important

**Choose Provincial (BC) When**:
- Operating primarily in BC
- No Canadian resident directors available
- Simpler compliance preferred
- Lower profile desired

### Cost Comparison

| Item | Federal | BC |
|------|---------|-----|
| Incorporation | $200 | $352 |
| Name search | ~$50 | $32 |
| Annual return | $12 | $44 |
| Compliance complexity | Medium | Lower |
