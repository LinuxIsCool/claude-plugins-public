## Key Findings Summary

### 1. Singularity Vectors
- **Agent Memory** is the fastest-growing area and most likely singularity contribution path
- **Temporal reasoning** (bi-temporal KGs) enables historical understanding
- **Federation** is the unsolved problem blocking collective intelligence

### 2. Critical Gaps
- **Standardization failure**: No Schema.org equivalent for AI knowledge
- **Complexity barrier**: Cypher/SPARQL harder than SQL
- **Privacy/federation tension**: Individual knowledge vs collective intelligence

### 3. Highest Leverage Interventions
1. Shift paradigm: KGs as infrastructure, not application
2. Coordinate standardization: Incentivize schema convergence
3. Improve DX: Make KG as easy as SQL

### 4. 35% Probability Assessment
35% chance of runaway positive feedback loop in next 5 years, contingent on:
- Solving standardization (R4 loop currently negative)
- Breaking complexity barrier (B1 loop mitigation)
- Federation protocols emerging
